              \begin{enumerate}
                  \item The angle formed by the lateral edge and the base of the pyramid. \sol{} Let
                        the foot point of the pyramid be $E$.

                            \text{In } ABCD,\ AB & = \sqrt{AD^2 + BD^2} \\
                                                 & = \sqrt{8^2 + 8^2}   \\
                                                 & = \sqrt{128}cm       \\
                                                 & = 8\sqrt{2}          \\
                            AE                   & = \frac{AB}{2}       \\
                                                 & = 4\sqrt{2}cm        \\
                        \end{flalign*}
